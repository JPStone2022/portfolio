Thank you for visiting Julian Stone's portfolio website. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and protect any personal information you may provide while using this site.

1. Information I Collect

The primary way I collect personal information on this site is through the Contact Form. When you use the contact form, you may provide:

  • Name: To know who is contacting me.
  • Email Address: To respond to your inquiry.
  • Subject & Message: The content of your message, query, or feedback.

This website does not currently use tracking cookies for analytics (like Google Analytics) or advertising. Necessary cookies might be used by the web framework (Django) for basic site functionality like security (CSRF protection) but are not used to track your activity across other sites.

2. How I Use Your Information

The personal information collected via the contact form is used solely for the purpose of:

  • Responding to your inquiries, comments, or requests.
  • Communicating with you regarding potential collaborations or opportunities if initiated by you.

I do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others.

3. Data Storage and Security

I take reasonable technical measures to protect the information submitted via the contact form. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

  • Contact form submissions may be stored temporarily in email logs or on the hosting platform (e.g., PythonAnywhere, Render) as part of the standard operation of sending/receiving email or processing web requests.
  • I will retain the information only as long as necessary to address your inquiry or for potential follow-up communication initiated by you.

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, such as the right to access, correct, or request deletion of the data I hold about you (primarily data submitted via the contact form).

To exercise these rights, please contact me using the details provided below or via the contact form itself. I will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.
